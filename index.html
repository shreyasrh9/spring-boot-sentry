  • Before starting anything, please sign up for Sentry and login.
  • After logging in, this is how your screen will look like.


  • Click on New Project and create the project.


  • Click on get your dsn and you will get your dsn and make a note of it.


  • And that's it, you have got your dsn and you need to paste this dsn in your log4j.properties file.
    log4j.appender.SentryAppender.dsn= Your DSN here
  • After executing this application you will recieve an email similar to this.

  • Additionally you can handle and track the reported error. Sentry provides several options like STATUS, HAS TAG, ASSIGNED TO, BOOKMARKED BY, APPLICATION etc.
